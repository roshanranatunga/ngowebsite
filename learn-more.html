The Lanka Foundation for Social and Educational Research (LFSER) is an independent, non-profit organisation committed to advancing high-quality social and educational research in Sri Lanka. We conduct rigorous, context-sensitive studies that inform teaching and learning, support evidence-based policy, and address key social issues related to equity, access, and development. Through research, professional capacity building, and policy engagement, LFSER works with educators, institutions, and development partners to generate knowledge that contributes to inclusive and sustainable educational and social progress.
